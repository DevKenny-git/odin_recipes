Spaghetti Sauce with Ground Beef

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper

Steps:

  1. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic and green pepper in a large saucepan. Cook and stir until meat is brown and vegetables are tender. Drain grease.
  2. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano, basil, salt and pepper. Simmer Spaghetti sauce for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
