Efo Riro Recipes

Ingredients

  • Vegetable (Efo)
  • Palm oil
  • Grinded Pepper
  • Beef
  • Condiment
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put a clean pot on fire and allow the water in it to dry
  2. Pour some measure of palm oil into the pot and allow to heat for some minutes
  3. Add Pepper, seasoning and salt in moderate quantity
  4. Allow to cook for some minutes
  5. Dice the vegetable and wash it in water
  6. Rinse the vegetable to drain out the water
  7. Check if the stew is steamed, then add boiled or fried meat
  8. Allow to cook for about 8 more minutes
  9. Add the vegetable into the stew and cover for about three minutes
  10. Stir vigorously so the stew and the vegetable is well mixed
  11. Serve with any swallow or morsel of your choice
